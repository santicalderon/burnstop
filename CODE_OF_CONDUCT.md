# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple version of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).

## In short

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Critique code, not people.
- Assume good faith — every reporter here is trying to ship something that doesn't burn money.
- No harassment, no off-topic political fights, no spam.

## Enforcement

Maintainer reads every comment. Anyone who can't keep these in mind gets a warning, then a block. Reports: open an issue or email the address in `AGENT_CARD.md`.

## Scope

Issues, PRs, Discussions, any project communication channel.
